DE-IDENTIFICATION PROCESS:
-------------------------
All samples have been modified to remove:
- Institution names (replaced with generic terms like "college", "campus")
- Specific organization names (replaced with descriptive terms)
- Personal names (removed or generalized)
- Geographic locations (removed when not essential to content)
- Course codes and specific class identifiers

WRITING TASK:
------------
All samples were produced in response to the following prompt:
"Describe a valuable experience you have had during your college career."

Time limit: 5 minutes
Assessment: Writing Quality Scale for Higher Education (WQS-HE)

QUALITY SCORE RANGES:
--------------------
High Quality: ≥19.4 (scores range from 19.4 to 21.6)
Medium Quality: 17.0-19.3 (scores range from 17.0 to 19.3)
Low Quality: <17.0 (scores range from 10.6 to 16.9)

Total possible score range: 6-30 (based on WQS-HE rubric)
